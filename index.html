Hello, and welcome to my book review website. Here I will review several books across several genres of reading. The books reviewed include The Way of Kings by Brandon Sanderson, an epic fantasy book, The Taking of Jake Livingston by Ryan Douglass, a YA book about a boy who sees the dead, and The Bright Ages by Matthew Gabriele and David M. Perry, a history book focused on reexamining the way the Middle Ages are seen historically.

Each book will have a rating out of ten, as well as a list of pros and cons at the bottom. The pros and cons will serve as a shortened version of the review itself.
